Project Criteria

We are a comprehensive infrastructure company. We are focused on medium and large scale infrastructure projects, typically underpinned by long-term, take-or-pay contracts. Our core competency is in renewable energy, where our founders have considerable experience. In addition, we have also advised on pipeline, conventional generation, and utility transactions. We work actively in both development and financing with partners and investors on projects over $20mm USD in the following areas.

  • Partners in Development (these will be clickable)

    In development, we work closely with a variety of organizations to build critical infrastructure. We are partnered with local and international NGO's, construction companies, environmental experts, government agencies, and other project developers. We help these organizations advance projects to a stage where foreign investors will finance construction.

  • Partners in Financing (these will be clickable)

    In financing, we want to help good capital find good projects. We work with sophisticated investors to help them identify, analyze, and invest in global infrastructure projects. We work with financial buyers, strategic buyers, impact investors, and family offices.
